The Challenges We Face

Every day, citizens encounter infrastructure issues that disrupt daily life. The reporting process is often tedious and lacks transparency.

Potholes & Roads

Damaged streets causing accidents and traffic jams.

Water Supply

Irregular water supply and pipeline leakages.

Waste Management

Uncollected garbage and overflowing public bins.

Street Lighting

Non-functional streetlights compromising public safety.

Why Choose JanSetu?

Empowering both citizens and authorities with state-of-the-art tools to build better cities together.

AI-Powered Reporting

Our smart system automatically categorizes your issues and identifies duplicates to streamline the resolution process.

Multilingual Support

Report issues in your regional language. We break down the language barrier between citizens and government.

Total Transparency

Track your complaint status in real-time. Know exactly which department and officer is handling your request.

Accountability

SLAs (Service Level Agreements) ensure that authorities resolve complaints within a designated timeframe.

How It Works

A seamless journey from identifying an issue to its final resolution.

1

Report

Citizen captures a photo, adds details, and submits the complaint easily.

2

Analyze

AI categorizes the issue and routes it to the correct government department.

3

Assign

4

Resolve

The issue is fixed, and the citizen gets notified with a completion proof.
